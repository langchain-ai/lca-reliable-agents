# OfficeFlow Supply Co. - Returns & Refunds Policy

## RETURN ELIGIBILITY

Items may be returned within **60 days of delivery**. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. All returns require an RMA number before shipping. Unopened products in original packaging are eligible for return as well as defective items. However, opened paper reams cannot be returned due to hygiene standards, and custom-printed items are also ineligible. Products marked as Final Sale cannot be returned under any circumstances, and items without packaging are not accepted.

**IMPORTANT EXCEPTION:** Defective or damaged items can be returned at any time, even after the 30-day window has passed. Items shipped in error are also always eligible.

## RETURN PROCESS STEPS

Contact our Returns Department first. You must provide your order number and explain why you're returning the item. They will then issue you a number that you'll need. This number is called a **Return Merchandise Authorization** or **RMA**. It's required for all returns without exception. The RMA is valid for 14 days from issue.

When shipping items back, the RMA number must be clearly marked on the outside of the package. Use original packaging when possible and include your packing slip. We recommend using insured shipping with tracking for your protection.

**Contact information:** returns@officeflow.com or phone 1-800-OFFICE-1 ext. 3. Response time is within 4 business hours during business days.

## REFUND PROCESSING

Refunds are processed within **5-7 business days**. The original payment method will be credited. Important note: shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or the item is defective. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility in most cases unless the item was defective or we shipped the wrong item.

## DEFECTIVE ITEMS

If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact us immediately. Within 48 hours of delivery is preferred but not required. Provide photos of the damage if possible so we can document the issue. We will arrange for pickup or send you a prepaid return label at no cost. Replacement items are shipped immediately upon confirmation of the defect. You will not be charged for return shipping on defective items.

## EXCHANGES

We're happy to exchange items. The same process as returns applies - you need an RMA. Price differences may apply if exchanging for a different item. Exchange items are shipped once we receive and inspect the return.

## BUSINESS ACCOUNTS

Business customers may have customized return policies. These are based on their specific agreement with OfficeFlow. Contact your dedicated account manager for details about your return policy.

For questions, contact our Returns Department. Email returns@officeflow.com (preferred for detailed requests) or phone 1-800-OFFICE-1 ext. 3. Hours are Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST.
