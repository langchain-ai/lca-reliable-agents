# OfficeFlow Supply Co. - Ordering & Payment Policy

## HOW TO ORDER

OfficeFlow offers multiple ordering methods for your convenience. **Online Web Portal** is the recommended method and is available 24/7. You can access it at www.officeflow.com and it provides real-time inventory visibility, order history, tracking, saved shopping lists, favorites, and digital invoices.

**Phone Orders** can be placed through our Sales Team. Call 1-800-OFFICE-1 ext. 2 for assistance. Hours are Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM EST. Saturday hours are 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM EST only. We are closed on Sundays and during major holidays.

**Email Orders** are accepted at sales@officeflow.com and should include item numbers or descriptions, quantities desired, and delivery address. During business hours we respond within 2 business hours to all email orders.

**Account Manager** service is available for Business Accounts. This provides dedicated support if you are a high-volume customer. Custom pricing and payment terms are available through this channel. Contact accounts@officeflow.com to set up an account manager relationship.

## MINIMUM ORDERS

There is a minimum order value that applies to most customers. This minimum is **$50 before tax and shipping** are calculated. However, customers with established business accounts do not have any minimum order requirements. Bulk pricing is available on many items if you need large quantities - contact sales for quotes.

## PAYMENT METHODS ACCEPTED

**Credit and Debit Cards:** We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. All cards are processed securely through our encrypted checkout system. Cards are charged when your order ships, not when you place the order.

**Business Accounts:** Payment terms are available for qualified businesses. Net 30 payment terms require credit approval first. Some qualified accounts can get Net 60 terms if they meet certain criteria. Monthly statements are emailed by the 5th of each month to the billing contact. Early payment discounts are available - ask your account manager about these opportunities.

**Purchase Orders:** These are accepted from established business accounts only. Every order must include a PO number and you must reference this PO number for any related inquiries or issues.

**Other Methods:** Check or money order can be mailed to our payment processing center. The address is OfficeFlow Supply Co., PO Box 4321, Portland, OR 97208. Wire transfer is available for large orders - contact accounting@officeflow.com for wire transfer details and instructions.

## PRICING INFORMATION

Current pricing is displayed on our web portal and can be provided by the sales team when you call. Bulk discounts are available for larger orders. Typically this means 10 or more units of the same item. Contract pricing is available for regular high-volume customers who order frequently. Quote requests should be sent to sales@officeflow.com or you can call the sales team directly. All quotes are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.

## ORDER CONFIRMATIONS

An email confirmation is sent immediately upon order placement to the email address on file. This confirmation includes your order number, the items ordered, quantities, and estimated delivery date. If you need any changes, contact us within 2 hours of placing the order. After processing begins, cancellations may incur restocking fees.

## MODIFICATIONS AND CANCELLATIONS

**Before Processing:** If you contact us within 2 hours of ordering, full modifications and cancellations are available with no fees or penalties. Contact the sales team immediately if you need to make changes.

**After Processing:** Once processing has started, cancellation may incur a 15% restocking fee on the order total. Modifications are limited to delivery address changes only after processing begins. Custom or special-order items cannot be cancelled under any circumstances once processing has started.

## BACKORDER POLICY

If an item is temporarily out of stock when you order it, we will notify you of the expected availability date. You can choose to wait for the item to come back in stock or substitute with a similar product that we have available. You can cancel backorders anytime without any penalty. You are not charged until the backordered item actually ships to you.

## SPECIAL ORDERS

Custom quantities and special requests are welcome at OfficeFlow. Typical lead time for special orders is 1-2 weeks from approval. Minimum quantities may apply depending on the item and supplier. A non-refundable deposit may be required for some special orders. Contact sales@officeflow.com with your specific requirements.

## BUSINESS ACCOUNT BENEFITS

Setting up a business account provides several benefits including Net 30 or Net 60 payment terms, no minimum order requirements, a dedicated account manager for your company, custom pricing for regular orders, consolidated monthly billing statements, and purchase order processing.

To apply for a business account, complete the online application at www.officeflow.com/business or email accounts@officeflow.com for assistance. You will need to provide your business name, tax ID, and trade references. A credit check is required but it's a soft inquiry that won't affect your credit score. Approval typically takes 1-2 business days.
