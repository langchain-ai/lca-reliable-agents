# OfficeFlow Supply Co. - Company Information

## COMPANY HISTORY AND BACKGROUND

OfficeFlow Supply Co. was established in **1998** and has been operating for over 25 years. We have been a trusted partner for small-to-medium businesses. Our coverage area includes all of North America. We focus on paper products and office supplies distribution. Our core strengths are reliability, quality, and exceptional customer service that sets us apart from competitors.

## FACILITIES AND LOCATIONS

Our headquarters is located in **Portland, Oregon**. We also operate multiple distribution centers to serve our customers efficiently. These centers are strategically positioned in Chicago, Atlanta, and Toronto. This network allows us to serve customers throughout both the United States and Canada. Fast and efficient delivery is possible because of this strategic positioning.

## MISSION STATEMENT

We empower businesses to focus on what they do best. We do this by providing reliable, high-quality office supplies. Exceptional service is at the core of everything we do. Every interaction is an opportunity to build trust. We view customer relationships as partnerships, not transactions.

## CORE VALUES

**Customer First** means your success is our priority in all decisions. **Quality Matters** because we stand behind every product we sell to customers. **Sustainability** represents our commitment to eco-friendly practices and products that help the environment. **Reliability** means consistent service you can count on day after day. **Innovation** drives us to always seek better ways to serve you and improve our operations.

## COMPANY SIZE AND REACH

We currently employ over **150 employees**. These employees are dedicated to serving our customers across multiple departments. We process over **50,000 orders per year** which demonstrates our scale. Our customer base includes more than **3,000 active business customers**. These customers trust us for their office supply needs on an ongoing basis.

## AWARDS AND RECOGNITION

In 2023 we received the **Green Business Award**. This was for Sustainable Practices in our operations and product selection. In 2022 we were named **Best Office Supplies Distributor**. This recognition came from SMB Magazine in their annual awards. We have maintained an **A+ Rating with the Better Business Bureau**. This rating has been consistent since 2010 which shows our commitment to customer satisfaction.
