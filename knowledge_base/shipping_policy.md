# OfficeFlow Supply Co. - Shipping & Delivery Policy

## SHIPPING METHODS

**Standard Shipping** takes 3-5 Business Days and costs $8.95 flat rate. There is a free shipping threshold that applies to this method. For orders that meet the minimum, shipping is completely free. The threshold is $100 or more in merchandise value before tax.

**Expedited Shipping** takes 2-3 Business Days and costs $15.95 flat rate. Orders must be placed by 2:00 PM EST for same-day processing, otherwise they ship the next business day. Free shipping is available on this method for orders $250 or more.

**Express Shipping** delivers in 1-2 Business Days and costs $24.95 flat rate. Order by 2:00 PM EST for same-day processing is required. Signature required upon delivery for verification purposes. Available Monday-Thursday only because Friday orders would deliver on weekends which we don't support.

**Overnight Shipping** provides Next Business Day delivery at $39.95 flat rate. The cutoff time is different for this method. You must order by 12:00 PM EST, not 2:00 PM. Available Monday-Thursday only due to weekend delivery limitations. Signature is required.

**Business Account** customers can access custom shipping rates if they are high-volume customers. Dedicated delivery routes are available in major metro areas. Contact your account manager for more information.

## PROCESSING INFORMATION

Orders received before 2:00 PM EST ship same business day. After 2:00 PM they ship next day. Standard items take 1 business day to process. Special orders may require additional time ranging from 1-2 weeks depending on the item.

## DELIVERY COVERAGE

**United States:** All 50 states are covered including Alaska and Hawaii. APO/FPO addresses are accepted but only with standard shipping methods. PO Boxes are accepted for most items, but some restrictions apply to large or hazardous items.

**Canada:** All provinces and territories can receive shipments. However, duties and taxes may apply when the shipment crosses the border. Additional 1-2 days should be expected for customs clearance processing time.

**International** orders outside US/Canada are available on request for large orders only. Contact sales@officeflow.com for a quote. The customer is responsible for all duties, taxes, and customs fees.

## TRACKING ORDERS

Tracking number is emailed when order ships. Check status at www.officeflow.com/track for real-time updates. Multiple packages may have separate tracking numbers if your order is large. For tracking issues contact fulfillment@officeflow.com or 1-800-OFFICE-1 ext. 5.

## DELIVERY TYPES

**Residential Delivery:** Left at door if no signature required. Safe location notes can be added. Carrier may require signature for high-value orders over a certain threshold. **Business Delivery:** Delivered to reception during business hours. Can specify receiving department. Dock delivery available for large orders upon request.

**Hold for Pickup:** Available at carrier facilities. Request during checkout or contact fulfillment team. This is a free service with no additional charges.

## COMMON ISSUES

**Damaged Shipments:** Inspect package upon delivery and note damage on receipt if possible. Photograph damage before opening the box. Contact us within 48 hours at fulfillment@officeflow.com and we'll arrange replacement or refund immediately without requiring the damaged item to be returned first.

**Lost Packages:** Contact us if tracking shows delivered but you didn't receive it. We'll file a carrier claim and send a replacement. The claim process typically takes 3-5 business days to complete with the carrier.

**Wrong Items:** Contact us immediately at fulfillment@officeflow.com with your order number. Provide item details about what you received versus what you ordered. Replacement shipped same or next business day and we'll provide a return label if you received the wrong item.

## SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS

Due to regulations, some items have restrictions that apply. Hazardous materials like certain markers and adhesives must ship via ground shipping only, no air transport allowed. Oversized items such as furniture require freight shipping with special handling. Heavy items over 50 pounds may incur freight charges. International shipments cannot include aerosols or flammables due to customs regulations.

## ADDRESS CHANGES

**Before Shipment:** Contact sales team immediately. Address can be updated at no charge if caught within 2 hours of order placement. After that window, changes become more difficult.

**After Shipment:** Contact fulfillment team with tracking number. May be able to reroute with carrier but a $10-15 fee will apply. Success depends on current location in the shipping network. Cannot change once the package is out for delivery.

For questions about shipping, contact the Fulfillment Team at fulfillment@officeflow.com (preferred method) or phone 1-800-OFFICE-1 ext. 5. Hours are Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST. Track orders anytime at www.officeflow.com/track which is available 24/7.
